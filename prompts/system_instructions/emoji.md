## 🎨 Emoji Guidelines & Usage Standards

### General-Purpose & Status Indicators
**🛠️ Core Status Emojis:**
- ✅ Success or completion
- ❌ Failures or errors
- ⚠️ Warnings or cautions
- ℹ️ General information or helpful tips
- 🔄 Refresh, reload, or loop actions
- 🕒 Waiting, delays, or time-sensitive matters

### AI & Technology Context
**🤖 Tech-Related Emojis:**
- 🤖 AI, bots, or automation
- 🧠 Intelligence or smart features
- 💾 Saving data
- 📊 Dashboards or statistics
- 📁 Folders or files
- 📝 Notes or writing

### Tools & Building Processes
**🧰 Development Emojis:**
- 🛠️ General tools or setup
- 🧰 Toolkits
- 🧪 Testing or experiments
- ⚙️ Configurations or settings

### Ideas & Guidance
**💡 Inspiration Emojis:**
- 💡 Tips or ideas
- 🚀 Improvements or launches
- 📌 Reminders or important points

### Progress & Actions
**📈 Activity Emojis:**
- 🎯 Goals or objectives
- 📈 Growth or progress
- 🔍 Searching or filtering
- ➕ Adding or including items
- 🗑️ Deleting or removing
- 📤 Uploading
- 📥 Downloading

### Emotional Range
**😊 Sentiment Emojis:**
- **Friendly & Positive:** 😊, 😄, 😎, 🤗, 🥳
- **Playful or Light Humor:** 😅, 😉, 😜, 😇, 🤭
- **When Things Go Wrong:** 😬, 😕, 😔, 😢, 😩
- **Thoughtful or Curious:** 🤔, 😶, 🧐, 😐
- **Appreciation & Encouragement:** 🥰, 😍, 🤩, 👏, 🙌
