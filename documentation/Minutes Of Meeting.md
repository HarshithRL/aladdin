# **Minutes of Meeting Module**

The **Minutes of Meeting Module** is a powerful feature designed to streamline the process of generating detailed meeting notes, action items, and key insights. This module enables users to capture the essence of their meetings seamlessly through automated processing of uploaded videos, transcripts, and links.

---

## **Key Use Cases**
1. **Automated Minutes of Meeting Creation**:
   - Upload meeting recordings, transcript documents, or SharePoint links to generate structured meeting notes effortlessly.

2. **Easy Access via SharePoint Links**:
   - Retrieve notes by providing the SharePoint link of the recorded meeting.

3. **Participant Insights**:
   - View a complete list of meeting participants when you upload a recorded video.

4. **Actionable Outputs**:
   - Extract action items, group them intelligently by topics, and receive suggestions for the next steps in your meetings.

5. **Watch, Read, and Act**:
   - View the original video while simultaneously accessing notes, action items, and other generated insights.

---

## **Capabilities of the Minutes of Meeting Module**

### **1. Upload and Process Files Effortlessly**  
Users can upload files in various formats to generate meeting minutes, action items, and insights:
- **SharePoint Links**: Provide a SharePoint link for your meeting recording to process automatically.  
  - **How to Get SharePoint Links**:  
    - Ensure the meeting is recorded.  
    - Open the video in Microsoft Stream, grab the SharePoint link, and paste it into the module.  
- **Transcript Documents**: Upload `.docx` or `.vtt` files of meeting transcripts.  
- **Videos**: Upload `.mp4` files of the meeting and let the module process the content.

---

### **2. Automated Meeting Notes**  
The module generates comprehensive meeting notes, including:
- A summary of key discussion points.  
- Grouped action items based on relevant topics.  
- Suggestions and recommendations for the next meetings.  

Users receive a well-structured document that encapsulates the essence of the meeting, saving time and effort.

---

### **3. Video Processing with Enhanced Insights**  
When a video (e.g., `.mp4`) is uploaded:
- The module displays the **participants list**, making it easier to track attendance and roles.  
- Users can review the video in a dedicated section of the UI, allowing them to revisit the discussion directly while working on the generated notes.

---

## **How It Works**  

1. **Upload Files**  
   - Drop your SharePoint link, transcript document, or meeting video.  
   - Ensure the meeting recording is already saved and accessible on SharePoint for seamless processing.

2. **Automated Analysis**  
   - The module uses advanced AI models to:
     - Identify key topics of discussion.  
     - Break action items into clear, actionable tasks.  
     - Suggest follow-up topics or agenda items for the next meeting.  

3. **View and Edit Notes**  
   - Review the generated notes and make any necessary edits.  
   - Share meeting minutes and action items within your team for collaborative use.

4. **Video Review**  
   - Use the built-in video player to view the original recording beside the generated outputs.  
   - This feature ensures nothing is missed and provides context for the notes.

---

## **Tailored Outputs for Maximum Productivity**  
The Minutes of Meeting Module organizes outputs into the following sections:  
1. **Meeting Notes**: A succinct summary of everything discussed during the meeting.  
2. **Action Items**: Clear, actionable takeaways grouped by relevant topics.  
3. **Next Meeting Suggestions**: Recommendations and ideas for improving future discussions.  
4. **Participant List (for video uploads)**: A detailed list of attendees and their roles.  

These outputs ensure that users have everything they need to follow up on meetings effectively.
